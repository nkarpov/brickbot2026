# DAIS 2026 Venue Information

## Location

**Moscone Center**
747 Howard Street
San Francisco, CA 94103

## Conference Dates

June 15-18, 2026 (Monday-Thursday)

## Buildings

The conference spans multiple buildings:

- **Moscone West** - Main sessions, keynotes, expo hall
- **Moscone South** - Workshops, hands-on labs
- **Moscone North** - Registration, partner pavilion

## Room Locations

### Keynote Hall
- Location: Moscone West, Level 3
- Capacity: 5,000 attendees
- All keynotes streamed to overflow rooms

### Session Rooms
- Rooms 2001-2024: Moscone West, Level 2
- Rooms 3001-3016: Moscone West, Level 3
- Workshop Labs: Moscone South, Level 1

## Parking

- **Moscone Center Garage**: 255 Third Street (underneath Moscone West)
  - Daily rate: $45
  - Validation available at registration desk
  
- **Fifth and Mission Garage**: 833 Mission Street
  - Daily rate: $35
  - 5-minute walk to venue

## Public Transit

- **BART**: Powell Street Station (15-minute walk) or Montgomery Station (10-minute walk)
- **Muni**: Lines 14, 30, 45 stop nearby
- **Caltrain**: 4th & King Station (15-minute walk or Muni connection)

## Hotels

Official conference hotels with shuttle service:
- Marriott Marquis (connected to Moscone)
- W San Francisco (2 blocks)
- InterContinental (3 blocks)
- Hilton Union Square (5 blocks)
