# DAIS 2026 Policies

## Code of Conduct

All attendees, speakers, sponsors, and staff must follow our Code of Conduct:

- Be respectful and inclusive
- No harassment of any kind
- No discriminatory language or behavior
- Report concerns to staff (look for "STAFF" badges)

Violations may result in removal from the conference without refund.

## Badge Policy

- Badges required for entry to all conference areas
- Badges are non-transferable
- Lost badges: Visit registration desk with photo ID
- Badge types:
  - **Full Conference** (blue) - All sessions, expo, meals
  - **Expo Only** (green) - Expo hall and keynotes only
  - **Speaker** (gold) - Full access + speaker lounge
  - **Staff** (red) - Conference staff

## Photo & Video Consent

- Professional photographers present throughout
- Photos may be used for marketing
- "No Photo" stickers available at registration if you prefer not to be photographed
- Session recording: Some sessions recorded for later viewing

## Food & Dietary Restrictions

- Breakfast: 7:30-9:00 AM (Moscone West, Level 2)
- Lunch: 12:00-1:30 PM (Moscone West, Expo Hall)
- Afternoon snacks: 3:00-4:00 PM

Dietary options:
- Vegetarian clearly labeled
- Vegan options available
- Gluten-free options available
- Kosher/Halal: Contact catering@dais2026.com in advance
