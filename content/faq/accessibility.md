# DAIS 2026 Accessibility Information

## Mobility

- All conference areas are wheelchair accessible
- Elevators located in all buildings
- Accessible restrooms on every level
- Reserved seating at front of session rooms
- Wheelchair rentals: Available at registration (first-come)

## Hearing

- Sign language interpretation: Available for keynotes and select sessions
- Request interpretation: accessibility@dais2026.com (7 days advance notice)
- Assistive listening devices: Available at registration desk
- Live captioning: All keynotes and main stage sessions

## Vision

- Service animals welcome
- Large-print materials: Request at registration
- Braille materials: Limited availability, request in advance

## Quiet Spaces

- Wellness room: Moscone West, Level 1, Room 105
- Low-sensory environment
- Available for breaks, meditation, or rest

## Dietary & Medical

- Allergen information: Posted at all food stations
- Refrigeration for medication: Available at First Aid
- Private nursing room: Moscone West, Level 1, Room 106

## Contact

Accessibility Coordinator: accessibility@dais2026.com
Phone: +1 (415) 555-DAIS
On-site: Look for staff with "Accessibility" ribbons on badges
